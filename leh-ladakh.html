Explore the Mesmerizing Beauty of Leh Ladakh

Leh Ladakh is a region of incredible beauty, with its dramatic landscapes, rugged mountains, and serene monasteries. It’s an adventurer’s paradise and a photographer’s dream.

Package Details

Duration: 7 Days, 6 Nights

Major Attractions

  • Pangong Lake: Famous for its ever-changing colors and scenic beauty.
  • Khardung La Pass: One of the highest motorable roads in the world.
  • Shanti Stupa: A symbol of peace and a great spot for panoramic views of Leh.
  • Nubra Valley: Known for its dunes, monasteries, and Bactrian camels.

Things to Do

  • Go trekking along the rugged trails.
  • Experience the thrill of biking on high-altitude passes.
  • Visit the ancient monasteries for a spiritual retreat.
  • Enjoy stargazing under the clear Ladakhi skies.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Leh Ladakh is between **May and October**, when the roads are clear, and the weather is pleasant for outdoor activities.
