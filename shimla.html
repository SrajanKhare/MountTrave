Discover the Charm of Shimla

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is a picturesque hill station that retains its colonial charm. Surrounded by rolling hills and lush greenery, Shimla is a favorite among tourists looking to unwind and experience the cool mountain air.

Major Attractions

  • The Ridge: A large open space offering spectacular views of the surrounding hills.
  • Jakhoo Temple: A famous Hanuman temple situated on the highest peak in Shimla.
  • Kufri: A small hill station known for skiing and hiking trails.
  • Christ Church: The second oldest church in North India, renowned for its neo-Gothic architecture.

Things to Do

  • Take a leisurely stroll along Mall Road.
  • Go ice-skating during winters.
  • Experience toy train rides through scenic routes.
  • Explore the historical architecture of the British era.

Best Time to Visit

Shimla can be visited year-round, but the best time is during **March to June** and **December to February** for snow lovers.
