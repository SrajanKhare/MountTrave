Relax in the Serenity of Kasauli

Kasauli is a small, charming hill town in Himachal Pradesh, known for its lush green forests, quiet lanes, and colonial architecture. It’s the perfect destination for those seeking peace and tranquility.

Package Details

Duration: 4 Days, 3 Nights

Major Attractions

  • Manki Point: The highest point in Kasauli, offering beautiful views of the surrounding area.
  • Gilbert Trail: A scenic trail for nature lovers.
  • Kasauli Brewery: One of the oldest distilleries for Scotch whisky in Asia.
  • Christ Church: A beautiful Anglican church dating back to the British era.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kasauli is between **April to June** and **September to November**.
